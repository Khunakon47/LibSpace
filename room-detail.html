About this room

Study Room A1 is a comfortable and well-equipped space designed for small group study sessions and collaborative work. Located on the 3rd floor of the Central Library, this room offers a quiet environment with modern amenities including a high-quality projector and a large whiteboard. The room features ergonomic seating, ample natural lighting, and air conditioning for your comfort. Perfect for group projects, study sessions, and small presentations.
